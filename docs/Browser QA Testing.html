ISSUE‑1

8.7‑TC1

High

1) Log in as an Author and open any manuscript.<br>2) Click Check Consistency from the toolbar.<br>3) Wait for the consistency check to process.

Expected: The AI consistency check should run, showing a skeleton loader and opening a sidebar sheet listing issues. The sidebar should stay open and close only via its own controls.

Actual: After clicking Check Consistency, the button briefly shows Queued then changes to Check Failed. No skeleton loader or sidebar appears, and the check cannot be retried successfully.

Release Verdict

❌ Not Ready — The critical consistency check fails immediately, preventing all issue‑related workflows from functioning. This blocks the majority of Story 8.7 test cases (sidebar behavior, issue rendering, quick-fix workflow, filtering, accessibility, etc.), making the release unsuitable for production. Story 8.19 functions correctly for zero-token scenarios, but without a working AI check the dynamic token usage cannot be validated. Fixing the AI consistency check is necessary before release.
